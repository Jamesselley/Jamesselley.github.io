About Us

Presha records is an independent dance music record label based in Guildford.

Presha started off as a collective of DJs, artists and music enthusiasts, running frequent club nights in an area that was somewhat lacking in musical diversity. Priding ourselves in diversity, freedom and non-limitation, you can expect to hear a wide variety of sounds at Presha events, including: house, techno, disco, dubstep, garage and more.

Presha has seen many successes, and has booked the likes of Jackmaster, Mele, Paleman, Lamont, Commodo, Artwork and many more big name DJs. Our resident DJs have headlined and supported these events, striving to push the boundaries of sound and bring the cutting edge of music to the dance floors of Guildford.

After years of success running club nights through Presha, affiliates started 'Presha Records' as a platform to curate and bring together music from anyone involved with Presha. This includes those who have played at our events, participated, attended and supported us over the years. If you want to get involved or want to send us your demos then check out our contact page and get in touch!

Our main goal is to release forward-thinking dance music, inspired by the night we run, Presha.
