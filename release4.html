Skunks - U-505 EP [PRSH004]

The fourth artist-focused EP from Presha records comes courtesy of Skunks. Following Rinse FM support for his debut track Desolate (which appeared on Presha V/A 002 earlier this year), Skunks continues to develop and hone his dark, sparse, and eerie take on UK techno on his debut solo EP.

‘Rhythmically my main influences for this EP are the broken beat techno style of Swamp 81 artists such as Lamont, Boddika and Paleman. Sonically I like the futuristic sci-fi sounds you hear in the music of Tessier Ashpool (Liar, Mutual Friend, Le Dom). I’m also a big fan of the lo-fi house scene at the minute, taking inspiration here through sound design and mixing. The inspiration for the theme and sounds of this EP came from watching a war documentary on submarines’.
