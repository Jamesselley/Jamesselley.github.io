GLSTR - Arples EP [PRSH002]

With our release schedule well and truly underway, we are delighted to present PRSH002, GLSTR’s dark and brooding EP, Arples. Taking his cues from artists such as Sidney Charles, Monika Kruse, and Joseph Capriati, on Arples GLSTR forges a path between groove orientated Tech House and Dark Techno, always keeping his sleek productions focused chiefly on the dancefloor. GLSTR has spent many years honing his DJ and production skills, and this experience manifests itself throughout this release.

“I began producing remixes & writing original material under a different alias which then developed into a more concentrated approach of writing Techno & Tech House over the years, & subsequently the launch of GLSTR. As part of this, I started to perform live using all original material. From this I was able to develop & improve my production, as well as awareness for what worked well live, constantly comparing my own stuff to, and being influenced by what other DJs were playing. I always write as much as I can, constantly moving from project to project developing new ideas. I never want to settle really, that’s boring...the aim is to get a sound & push it as much as possible.”
