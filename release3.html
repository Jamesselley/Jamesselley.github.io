BETT - U Dance EP [PRSH003]

Budding producer BETT brings the dancefloor heat for his U Dance EP, the third release on Presha Records. Having formerly dabbled in producing breaks, BETT has found his home in crafting weird, wonky, upbeat and ultimately downright good fun house cuts, as showcased on this four- track release.
