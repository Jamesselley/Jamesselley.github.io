Release 4: Skunks U-505

The fourth artist-focused EP from Presha records comes courtesy of Skunks. Following Rinse FM support for his debut track Desolate (which appeared on Presha V/A 002 earlier this year), Skunks continues to develop and hone his dark, sparse, and eerie take on UK techno on his debut solo EP.

Click the image for more information on this release, featuring remixes from Presha residents Facet and 95Bones. Below is one of the tracks from the EP, titled 'Sub Warfare'.


Presha 11/11/17 BRASSICA

For our first Saturday show of the new year, we bring you Brassica!
Brassica is a UK producer who has worked expressively in many musical styles from real electro, italo disco and synth music through to techno and rave nostalgia. Brassica has had releases on Dissident, Civil Music and most recently an Acid, Breaks and Balmy House EP on the Feel My Bicep Label. Having an enviable list of supporters including Bicep, DJ Harvey, Maceo Plex and Trevor Jackson, we are delighted to welcome Brassica to The Basement!

If you are interested in coming please click on the image, which will to take you to the Facebook event page. The event will run from 23:00 - 3:00 in The Basement, Guildford (GU2 7XH).

The Resident Series #4 - Hyde

The fourth instalment of the 2017 Presha residents mix series comes from Hyde, bringing his unique blend of house, techno, disco, electro and everything in between!

"This mix features many of my favourite picks this month. I've tried to create a variety of vibes in this, whilst maintaining that sense of progression in tempo and intensity throughout the hour."
