95Bones - Blast Chiller EP [PRSH001]

Following on from our well received compilation released in late February, comes the first in our series of artist EPs. Beginning proceedings is 95Bones with his EP, Blast Chiller. 95Bones has been part of the Presha collective for 2 years, and in that time has developed a distinctive mixing style, blending heavy, powerful tracks with fun and light selections in order to create lighthearted sets that demand dancing from their audience. On Blast Chiller, 95Bones brings this approach to his productions, blending musical ideas with his eclectic and far ranging knowledge of dance music as a whole.
